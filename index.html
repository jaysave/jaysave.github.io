Water Source
Heat Pump Product

Led the mechanical and development of a packaged geothermal heat pump from concept through production. Work included sheet metal, injection molded plastic, bent copper tubing. Worked with a cross-functional team including multiple levels of contract manufacturers.

Public Transit
Custom Designs

Designed verious parts for public transit vehicles and wayside infrastucture. This includes bus and rail, internal and external parts. Designed custom mounting solutions for networking equipment, CCTV cameras, LIDAR, radio frequency antennas, and power distribution systems.

Solar Inverter Product

Designed and sustained solar inverter equipment manufacturing from new product introduction to legacy support for customers. Designed for and received UL certification marks for Solar inverters and string combiners including UL 1741 and NEMA4X rating for watertightness.

Vehicle Mounted Thermal Imaging System

Designed, built, and tested vehicle based thermal imaging system for driveby thermal surveying. Mounted 8 cameras, 2 LIDARs, GPS system, and various other sensors to develop a robotic system to survey buildings for heat loss continuously.
